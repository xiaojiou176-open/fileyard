## Local Photo Organizer With Review-First AI Assistance

Most photo organizers ask you to choose between two bad options: manual cleanup that takes forever, or automatic file mutation that starts moving images before you trust the plan.

Fileman is for the middle ground.

## Why Fileman Fits Photo Work

- Analyze drafts a manifest before files move
- Review Queue lets you inspect the proposal first
- Collection Intelligence groups one photo batch slice at a time
- Apply starts in dry-run mode
- Rollback stays available when a batch needs to be undone

## Good Fit

- trip or event folders that arrive in mixed batches
- camera-roll exports mixed with screenshots and PDFs
- local-first photo cleanup where auditability matters more than hands-free automation

## Not A Fit

- hosted photo management SaaS
- zero-review automation that silently renames and moves files
