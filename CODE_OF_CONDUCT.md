# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Treat this repository like a shared workshop: be respectful, be precise,
and keep the conversation focused on the work.

We expect contributors to:

- Respect other people and avoid personal attacks
- Debate the problem, not the person
- Share reproducible evidence instead of heat-only conclusions
- Minimize exposure when a discussion involves security, credentials, or
  privacy-sensitive information

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Posting secrets, private paths, or personal data in public threads
- Encouraging destructive or unauthorized operations when risk is known
- Repeatedly ignoring the repository's documented support and contribution
  boundaries

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove inappropriate content, close conversations,
or restrict participation when needed to keep collaboration safe and useful.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository issues, pull requests, review
comments, and maintainer-to-contributor interactions around this project.
